How to install in Outlook

1

Fill in your info and pick your signature version.

2

Click "Copy Signature" — this copies the formatted signature to your clipboard.

3

In Outlook, go to File → Settings → Account → Signatures.

4

Click "+ Add Signature" or ✏️ Edit an existing one. Select all (Ctrl+A) and paste (Ctrl+V).

How to install in NetSuite

1

Fill in your info and pick your signature version.

2

Click "Copy HTML" to copy the raw HTML code.

3

On the NetSuite homepage, under Settings, click "Set Preferences".

4

On the General tab, find the SIGNATURE box and paste the HTML code (Ctrl+V).

5

Check the "ADD SIGNATURE TO MESSAGES" box below the signature field.

6

Click the "Transactions" tab at the top.

7

Under TRANSACTION EMAIL ATTACHMENT FORMAT, select "PDF".

8

Click "Save".
